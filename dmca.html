📜 DMCA Policy

Hridoy PDF Tools respects the intellectual property rights of others. If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used or shared on our platform in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please contact us immediately.

To file a DMCA complaint, please provide:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Description of the copyrighted work
  • Exact URL or location of the content

Send all DMCA requests to our Contact page. We will review and take appropriate action promptly.
